Mark where you are now — your parked bike, a campsite, a meeting point. Later, an arrow points you back to it.

This page isn't on HTTPS, so the browser blocks GPS and compass access. Deploy it (Vercel, Netlify, GitHub Pages) or tunnel with ngrok, then open the https:// link.

Works outdoors with a clear view of the sky — GPS is accurate to roughly 3–15 m, so the arrow guides you close, then you look around. Indoors it will drift. Compass works best away from metal and magnets.
